Things to Do while visiting.

This is a plan of things to do while being in vaction for about a week in Rwanda, take a look at it if interested.

Monday & Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday Saturday
10:00am-12:00pm Kigali Genocide Memorial King’s Palace Museum Visit Local Markets Traditional Dance Performances Art Galleries
Coffee Plantation Tour Tea Plantation Visit Try Rwandan Cuisine Visit a Cultural Village Camping in Akagera
12:00pm-2:00pm Break time Attend Kwita Izina Hot Springs in Nyamyumba Visit the Rusumo Falls
2:00pm-3:00pm Explore Musanze Caves Explore the Twin Lakes Lake Kivu Boat Tour Hiking Mount Bisoke Canopy Walk
3:00pm-6:00pm Gorilla Trekking Birdwatching Golden Monkey Tracking Explore Rugezi Wetlands Visit the Umusambi Village
Chimpanzee Trekking Visit Inema Arts Center Horseback Riding in Musanze Explore the Rusizi River Go Paragliding Over Rwanda’s Hills
Enjoy!
